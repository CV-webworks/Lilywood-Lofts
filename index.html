About us

Specialist loft work with a hands-on, trusted team

Lilywood Carpentry is a family-run business based in East Barnet, North London. With nearly 20 years of experience, we specialise in loft conversions and high-quality carpentry, managing projects from start to finish and helping clients plan and design the loft space they have been imagining.

Our work spans North, West and East London, Hertfordshire and Essex. The approach is straightforward: clear advice, careful planning, honest communication and craftsmanship that holds up long after the project is complete. With Luke on site for all projects and around 80% of our work coming from recommendations, clients know exactly who they are working with and what standard to expect.
